Sustainability

We are committed to operating our business on 24/7 carbon-free energy by 2030, everywhere we operate.

From matching 100% of our electricity consumption with renewable energy purchases since 2017 to building the most energy-efficient data centers, sustainability is deeply embedded in our operations. Our goal is to leave a greener planet for the next generation.
