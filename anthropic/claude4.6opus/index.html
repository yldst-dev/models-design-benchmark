Safety

Safety is not
an afterthought

We believe building safe AI is not at odds with building capable AI. Safety is an integral part of our approach and drives every decision we make.

1

Responsible scaling. We test frontier models for dangerous capabilities before and during training, committing to pause if safety measures aren't adequate.

2

Interpretability research. We invest heavily in understanding the internal mechanisms of our models — not just what they do, but why.

3

Alignment by default. Our Constitutional AI approach builds helpfulness and harmlessness directly into the training process, not as guardrails bolted on afterward.

4

Transparency & collaboration. We publish our safety research openly and work with governments and institutions to establish responsible AI governance.
